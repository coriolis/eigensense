Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20-10-2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Eigensense handles your data. Our application processes camera input directly in your web browser and generates text descriptions locally on your device.

Data Collection and Processing

Camera Access

  • The application requires access to your device's camera
  • All camera footage is processed entirely within your web browser
  • No video or images are stored or transmitted from your device
  • Camera access is only active while the application tab is open and permission is granted

Text Descriptions

  • Text descriptions are generated locally on your device
  • Descriptions are only transmitted if you explicitly configure a Slack integration
  • If Slack integration is not configured, all generated text remains on your device

Local Storage

  • We store your application preferences (such as Slack configuration) in your browser's localStorage
  • This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers
  • You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings

Slack Integration (Optional)

  • If you choose to configure Slack integration:
    • Your provided Slack webhook URL is stored locally in your browser
    • Generated text descriptions are sent directly to your specified Slack channel
    • No data is processed through our servers; communication is directly between your browser and Slack
    • Slack's own privacy policy applies to data once it reaches their service

Third-Party Services

  • The only third-party service integration available is Slack (optional)
  • We recommend reviewing Slack's Privacy Policy if you enable this integration
  • No analytics or tracking tools are implemented

Data Storage

  • Only your preferences are stored locally using localStorage
  • No data is stored on remote servers
  • You can remove stored preferences at any time by clearing your browser data

Your Rights

You have complete control over:

  • Camera access (can be revoked through browser settings)
  • Local storage data (can be cleared through browser settings)
  • Slack integration (can be disabled or reconfigured within the app)

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. The latest version will always be available on this page.
